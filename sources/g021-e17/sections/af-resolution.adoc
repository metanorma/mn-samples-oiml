[appendix]
== Resolution of complaints and disputes

[[cls-F.1]]
=== {blank}
Complaints and disputes concerning the operation of the appropriate certification system
should be lodged with the appropriate designated body.

=== {blank}
The designating authority concerned should, upon request, assist in resolving a dispute
in <<cls-F.1>>.

=== {blank}
Complaints concerning prepackages should be forwarded with documented and substantiated
evidence to the responsible designated body for resolution in terms of its own procedures. Should
complaints not be satisfactorily resolved, the matter may be brought to the attention of the relevant
designating authority.

NOTE: This does not imply that the designating authority should be involved in legal proceedings.

=== {blank}
Complaints concerning designated bodies should be forwarded to the designating authority
concerned.
