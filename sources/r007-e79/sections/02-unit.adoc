== Unit of temperature and scale graduation

=== {blank}
The unit of temperature is the degree Celsius, symbol stem:["unitsml(degC)"].

[[cls-2.2]]
=== {blank}
The scale must extend at least from stem:[35.5 "unitsml(degC)"] to stem:[42.0 "unitsml(degC)"] with a scale interval of stem:[0.1 "unitsml(degC)"].
