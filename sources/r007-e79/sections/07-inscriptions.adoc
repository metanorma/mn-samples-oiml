[[cls-7]]
== Inscriptions

=== {blank}
The following inscriptions must be engraved or indelibly printed on the scalebearing strip
of enclosed-scale thermometers and on the stem of solid-stem thermometers:

==== {blank}
The symbol "stem:["unitsml(degC)"]" near the scale.

==== {blank}
The manufacturer's name or trademark.

[[cls-7.1.3]]
==== {blank}
An indication identifying the glass used for the bulb, if the glass is not already identified
by its maker.

=== {blank}
Other inscriptions may be added, but only if there is no risk of misleading the user.
