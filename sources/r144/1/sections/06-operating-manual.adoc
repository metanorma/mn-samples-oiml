
[[sec-6]]
== Operating manual

[[sec-6.1]]
=== {blank}

The operating manual shall be in the official language(s) of the country (or another
generally accepted language according to national legislation) and easily understandable.
An operating manual for users shall be made available for each individual instrument.

[[sec-6.2]]
=== {blank}

The operating manual shall include the following information:

. instructions for the correct operation of the instrument;
. maximum and minimum storage temperatures;
. rated operating conditions;
. warm-up time after switching on the instrument/electrical power;
. all other relevant mechanical and electromagnetic environmental conditions;
. mechanical and electromechanical environmental classes;
. safety and security conditions.
